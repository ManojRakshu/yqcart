What we do?

Welcome to yQcart! We redefine shopping,
yQcart.com is a portal where conventional and online shopping meet together. yQcart allow users the most convenient way to order grocery, food etc via mobile app. It saves time by avoiding you to wait in a queue. Users can give their pickup time or delivery time, so that it's ready on or before time. Users need to choose the store, what they want, add to cart, complete the payment and done. Oh yeah, it's free!
